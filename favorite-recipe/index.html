Fried Cheese Empanadas

With a sugar coated outershell hiding a cheesy interior, these empandadas are a delicious blend of sweet and savory.

Ingredients

  • Filling
    • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    • 1/4 cup minced white onion
    • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
    • quart vegetable oil
    • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Dough
    • 3 cups flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 1/2 cup milk

Preparation

  1. Dough
    1. Put the flour, salt, and baking powder into a bowl and mix.
    2. Cut the butter into small pieces and add it to the flour.
    3. mix until the mixture is crumbly and there are no large pieces of butter.
    4. Add the milk 2 tablespoons at a time and mix with a fork.
    5. Knead the dough until it is smooth.
    Wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and let it rest on the counter for 30 minutes (or refrigerate for several hours—up to overnight). Cover dough with plastic wrap and set aside for 30 minutes (or refrigerate until ready to use).
