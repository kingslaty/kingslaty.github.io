Fried Cheese Empanadas

With a sugar coated outershell hiding a cheesy interior, these empanadas are a delicious blend of sweet and savory.

Ingredients

  • Filling
    • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    • ¼ cup minced white onion
    • 1½ cups shredded cheese
    • one quart vegetable oil
    • ¼cup sugar
  • Dough
    • 3 cups all purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon baking powder
    • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • ½ cup milk

Preparation

  1. Prepare the Dough
    1. Put the flour, salt, and baking powder into a bowl and mix.
    2. Cut the butter into small pieces and add it to the mixture.
    3. Mix until the mixture is crumbly with no big pieces of butter.
    4. Add the milk 2 tablespoons at a time and mix with a fork.
    5. Knead the dough until it is smooth.
    6. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and leave to rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Filling
    1. Melt the butter on the stove over medium-low heat.
    2. Add the onion and cook until translucent and fragrant.
    3. Allow to cool and mix with the cheese.
  3. Shape the Empanadas
    1. Divide the dough into balls and roll out into a circle.
    2. Brush the edges lightly with water.
    3. Place a small amount of the filling into each circle.
    4. Fold the circles in half and close the seam by pressing it with a fork.
    5. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan large enough for frying over medium-high heat.
    6. Fry each empanada until golden brown.
    7. Place onto plate with a paper towel using tongs.
    8. Sprinkle sugar on top as soon as they are on the plate.
    9. Cool for five minutes and enjoy!
